New package `@geoalgeria/protection-civile` — Algeria's Protection Civile (civil protection / fire & rescue) units, official-primary from the DGPC's own dataset (dgpc.dz). 880 units nationwide with Arabic names, address/phone/fax and a status tier, all geocoded; wilaya re-derived by point-in-polygon against the 69 post-2026-reform boundaries (the DGPC's pre-reform `cod_wilaya` preserved in `refs.dgpc_wilaya`). JSON, CSV, GeoJSON, TypeScript.
